## Food Security

  
 The world is facing a crucial moment. In 2020, almost one third of people did not have enough food - hunger affected between 720 and 811 million people. Compared to 2019, 46 million more people in Africa, nearly 57 million more in Asia, and about 14 million more in Latin America and the Caribbean were experiencing hunger. Based on projections of global population growth, current shortfall in food production for the world, and increased demand for cleaner fuel & biodiesel, food security will continue to be a key economic development issue for the next several decades. As the conflict between food and fuel becomes more severe, there will be a time when more agricultural products will be used for energy than food. To complicate the situation, the COVID-19 pandemic has transformed the world in terms of supply chain, resource availability, and human labor and has revealed our weaknesses in food security even more. In short, humanity is at a critical point and what this extraordinary change in our lives has demanded from us - the agricultural experts and the technology specialists of the world - is to innovate and become more efficient to address the multiple challenges of food security.

## ENVIRONMENTAL, SOCIAL, AND GOVERNANCE (ESG) Platform

  
Hanumayamma Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Platform is a solution for dairy and agricultural farms, cooperatives, commercial corporations, manufacturing firms, multi-national corporations, and food processing units of different sizes to achieve their environmental sustainability goals and improve the world.   
  
 Hanumayamma Sustainability Platform collects data from different sensors that are deployed in the field, such as Cow Necklace, a Class 10 veterinary diagnostic sensor, and creates a shared data model. It then transforms various data to give insights on Water, Methane, Milk, and Green Analytics and helps farmers to use them.   
  
 This is how platform process data: first, it uses Hanumayamma Cloud for Sustainability data model, which enhances field sensor data, weather & satellite, and econometric data. Second, the data is analyzed with Hanumayamma’ s data science algorithms to produce ESG calculations and tracking for each tenant. Lastly, it produces customized data insights for compliance and governance reporting purposes. Actionable steps and progress towards objectives are the outcomes that help us & world achieve ESG goals.

#####  Winner of 2018 IEEE Big Data Cup - FEMH Voice Challenge

A high-quality microphone (Model: SM58, SHURE, IL) recorded voice samples of a 3-second sustained vowel sound /a:/ at a comfortable loudness level, with a distance of about 15–20 cm between the microphone and the mouth, using a digital amplifier (Model: X2u, SHURE) under a background noise level between 40 and 45 dBA. The sampling rate was 44,100 Hz with a 16-bit resolution, and data were stored in an uncompressed .wav format.
